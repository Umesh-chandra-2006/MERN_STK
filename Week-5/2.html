Jujutsu Kaisen

Introduction

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Gege Akutami. It follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the world of Jujutsu Sorcerers after swallowing a cursed object.

Profile

  1. Main Character - Yuji Itadori
  2. Strongest Sorcerer - Satoru Gojo
  3. Main Antagonist - Ryomen Sukuna
